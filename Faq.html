Savings

We have 5 savings plans;

  • Wallet savings: saving money on the wallet attract an interest of 10% per annum. With this plan, you can make withdrawal at any time but withdrawing more than 3 times in a month attracts a penalty of losing the interest for that month. Interest from any other saving plans are credited to the wallet which can be redeemed.
  • Reap Quick: on this savings plan, you can lock down funds for either 3, 6, 9, or 12months with a 12 – 12.5% interest per annum. You need to specify the duration of saving during the setting up of your plan and Reaprite will ensure the money is saved automatically to this plan. Withdrawing outside the set date attract a penalty fee of 2% on saving and interest for the month.
  • Reap Plus: this plan allow you to lock away funds for 6, 9 or 12months with a 12 – 14% interest per annum. No withdrawal is allowed except on maturity date.
  • Reap Max: with this plan funds are lock away for either 6 or 12months at a 14 -16% interest per annum. This plans help you meet commitments to long term financial goals.
  • Reap Goal: With reap goal you are saving for a particular goal to be achieved within a particular time.
